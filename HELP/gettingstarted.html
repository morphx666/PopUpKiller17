Getting Started

Right after finishing the installation process, select START->Programs->PopUp Killer->PopUp Killer, and it will appear in your tray area as yellow lightning icon (), (see figure 1)

PopUp Killer uses the icon in the tray area to show its current state, depending on various conditions:
Figure 1

Disabled
When PopUp Killer is disabled it will not monitor your system for possible popups.

Enabled
In this mode PopUp Killer is constantly monitoring for popups and closing those that are recognize by either the black list, the Smart! engine or the Limit mode.

Temporarily Disabled
You can temporarily disable PopUp Killer by holding the CTRL key down.
