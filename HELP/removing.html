Removing PopUps

For the PopUp's in the PopUps that will be closed list, you may either remove them from the list, or temporarily disable a selected PopUp.

To permanently remove a PopUp from the list:

  • Right click its name. A pulldown menu will appear.
  • Click the Remove option from the pulldown menu.

To temporarily disable a PopUp:

  • Clear its checkbox by clicking on it

To re-enable it:

  • Simply mark the checkbox by clicking it again
